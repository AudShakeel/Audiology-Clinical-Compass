Clinician dashboard for hearing and balance care

This platform is designed for audiologists, student clinicians, externs, preceptors, pediatric audiologists, CI audiologists, hearing aid clinicians, vestibular clinicians, educational audiologists, electrophysiology clinicians, and tinnitus or sound sensitivity clinicians.

Use it during real appointments to move from a patient concern to counseling language, printable patient education, red flags, outcome tools, referral guidance, and source-backed documentation. Restricted forms, questionnaires, test materials, proprietary manuals, and copyrighted figures are not reproduced. Clinicians are directed to official sources when permission or licensing review is needed.

How to use this in clinic

  1. Start with the patient concern or diagnosis.
  2. Open the matching specialty hub or clinical library card.
  3. Review red flags and referral guidance before counseling.
  4. Print a patient handout when appropriate.
  5. Use official links for questionnaires and restricted tools.
  6. Document using source-backed language and patient-specific findings.

Current launch structure

Copyright-safe model

Original patient education is hosted. Restricted questionnaires, scoring sheets, manuals, software screenshots, commercial speech materials, and copyrighted figures are not copied into the website.
