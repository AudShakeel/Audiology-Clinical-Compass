Audiology Clinical Compass Reviewer Brief

Created by: Tahreem Shakeel, AuD Candidate, University of the Pacific, San Francisco, CA.

This platform is designed to improve clinical efficiency by organizing hearing and balance patient handouts, outcome measure links, condition counseling resources, troubleshooting guidance, student/preceptor education, report templates, test battery decision support, and research translation pathways into one searchable clinician resource.

Reviewer questions

  1. Are the clinical categories useful for fast-paced audiology settings?
  2. Are any patient-facing handouts inappropriate, incomplete, or unclear?
  3. Are any citations, permissions, or legal safety labels insufficient?
  4. Which specialty sections need additional expert review before public launch?
  5. What should be prioritized next for clinical impact?
