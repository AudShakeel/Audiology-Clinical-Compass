Reviewer Brief

Project: The Audiology Clinician Resource Hub

Creator: Tahreem Shakeel, AuD Candidate, University of the Pacific, San Francisco, CA

Purpose: This site is designed as a clinician-only, evidence-linked, copyright-aware resource hub that supports efficient audiology counseling, troubleshooting, outcome measurement, student preparation, and specialty care.

Requested reviewer feedback

  1. Clinical accuracy and scope appropriateness.
  2. Content that should be removed, revised, or limited to official links.
  3. Missing subspecialty resources or outcome measures.
  4. Copyright and permissions concerns before public release.
  5. Ideas for institutional collaboration, student use, and quality improvement.

Launch safeguards

  • Restricted forms and commercial tests are not copied.
  • Original handouts include in-text source links and end references.
  • Medical red flags are included where relevant.
  • Every page has a last-reviewed date and a legal safety disclaimer.
